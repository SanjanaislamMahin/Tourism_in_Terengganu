TRANSPORTATION

Transportion of your choice where you feel safe & comfort

The two buses were named after two most popular tourism location in Terengganu, “Kenyir” and “Redang”.

“Town Bus ” begins it’s operation from 6 am in the morning until 10 pm

This bus will be a tourist latest attraction for visiting tourism locations around Kuala Terengganu

A lot of Trishaw transport found around Kuala Terengganu town especially in the town area for facilitating the public in traveling short distance without walking.

This facility is also provided with the aim of attracting tourists to visit Kuala Terengganu. The fare for trishaw service is also reasonable.

These trishaw transport normally starts operation from 8.00 in the morning until 6.30 in the evening every day.

A quite number of Taxi transports available around Kuala Terengganu town. This will facilitate the public to travel long distances.

Customers can also make early bookings to use the taxi service. Among other transport facilities found around Kuala Terengganu town is the jump bus.

These jump buses normally starts operation from 6.30 in the morning until 6.30 in the evening every day.

Journey around 6-7 hours, ticket can be purchase at Putra bus terminal. Company operate to Kuala Terengganu: TransNasional Tel: +603-4256 8218, Mutiara, SE warisan Sutra Express, KKKL, Kesatuan Express and Sani Express. Both (2-2) seating arrangement and comfortable 2-1(VIP) seating arrangement available.
